L25-single generates 1200×630 single-topic post covers from a YAML spec. The visual: field selects one of these 8 hand-crafted SVG primitives. Use this sheet to pick the right visual for your post archetype, then copy the YAML snippet directly into your spec file under _data/l25_covers/.
